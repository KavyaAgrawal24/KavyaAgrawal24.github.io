## Uranium Contamination Risk Mapping in India

A geospatial machine learning framework for predicting uranium contamination probability and translating model outputs into public-health and governance-relevant indicators.

**Key components:** risk probability mapping, transition regimes, population exposure, monitoring efficiency, governance blind spots, exposure inequality, policy priority zones.

## Fluoride Deep Learning Benchmarking

A multi-resolution deep learning experiment to compare model performance and variable sensitivity across spatial scales, including 250 m, 500 m, 1 km, 5 km, and 10 km resolutions.

## Arsenic Transferability and Interpretability

A modelling framework focused on temporal transferability, robustness, and explainable AI for arsenic contamination prediction.

## Geospatial Data Preparation for Groundwater AI

Workflows for harmonizing heterogeneous remote sensing, GIS, climate, soil, terrain, population, and hydrogeological datasets for model-ready geospatial analysis.
