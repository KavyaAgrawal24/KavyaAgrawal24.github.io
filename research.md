## Doctoral Research Theme

My Ph.D. research aims to build a standardized and adaptable AI/ML/DL and remote-sensing-based framework to predict and map groundwater contamination across heterogeneous hydrogeological and geographic settings in India.

## Core Research Questions

- How can multi-source environmental, hydrogeological, and remotely sensed datasets be integrated for groundwater contamination prediction?
- How do model outputs change across spatial scales and resolutions?
- Can predicted contamination probabilities be translated into exposure, governance, and policy-relevant indicators?
- How can spatial AI outputs support groundwater monitoring expansion and public-health-oriented planning?
